What are angel numbers?

Angel numbers are repeating or patterned sequences - 111, 717, 1044 - that keep showing up on clocks, receipts, and license plates until you finally notice. Many believe they are messages: a nudge from your guides, the universe, your own higher self. Type whatever number keeps finding you above, and Code Words decodes what it carries for your love, your work, your money, and your spirit - plus an affirmation to carry with you.
